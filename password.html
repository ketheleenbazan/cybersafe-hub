How to Create a Strong Password

  • Use at least 8 characters (the longer, the better!)
  • Include uppercase and lowercase letters
  • Add numbers and special symbols (like !, @, #, $)
  • Avoid using personal info (like your name or birthday)
  • Don’t use common words or easy patterns (like "password" or "1234")
