Encrypted Notes Vault

The Encrypted Notes Vault lets you securely store your private notes using strong encryption.
How does it work?

  • You choose a passphrase and type your note.
  • Your note is encrypted in your browser before it is saved.
  • Only someone with the correct passphrase can decrypt and read your notes.
  • Great for learning about real-world encryption and privacy!

Want to try it out and save your secure notes?
