About CyberSafe Hub

CyberSafe Hub is an easy-to-use website all about helping people stay safe online. It's built especially for folks who don't have a tech background or who sometimes feel a bit lost with all the online threats out there. The idea is simple: explain cyber safety in plain English, give practical tools, and make learning about security feel less scary and more doable.

On this site, you'll find simple guides, interactive games, and helpful tools that show you how to protect your personal info and avoid common online dangers.
