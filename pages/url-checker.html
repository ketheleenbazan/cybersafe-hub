Fake URL Detector

Enter a website address (URL) below to check for common signs of phishing or fake websites.


What to Look For in a URL:

  • Spelling: Are there typos in the company name? (e.g., "amaz0n.com" instead of "amazon.com")
  • Subdomains: Too many dots or strange words before the main domain? (e.g., "login.bank.secure.com.phishing.ru")
  • HTTPS: Does it start with "https://" and show a padlock? (Though not foolproof!)
  • IP Addresses: Does it use numbers instead of a name? (e.g., "http://192.168.1.100/login")
