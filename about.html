About CyberSafe Hub

CyberSafe Hub is an interactive cybersecurity awareness website designed for non-technical users and digital beginners. Our mission is to make online safety simple, practical, and accessible for everyone.

  • Learn how to create strong passwords
  • Spot phishing scams with real-world examples
  • Test your knowledge with fun quizzes

This project is open source and free to use for educational purposes.
Created as part of a digital literacy and cybersecurity education initiative.
